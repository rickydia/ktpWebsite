Open House

KTP rush begins with two open house events. Rushees are only required to attend one of the two events. During Open House, rushees and actives will get a chance to introduce themselves and get to know more about KTP over refreshments.

Attendance to one open house is required, dress is casual.

Location: Ricks

Dates: 1/1 & 1/2 @ 6 pm
