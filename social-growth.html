Lifelong Friends and Memories.

The people you meet in Kappa Theta Pi will go on to be some of your closest friends throughout college and beyond. Although we are a diverse group of individuals, we are connected by our passion and love for technology. We host a variety of social events throughout the semester, including but not limited to formal, tailgates, bowling, and apple picking.
