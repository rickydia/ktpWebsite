Lifelong Friends and Memories.

The people you meet in Kappa Theta Pi will go onto to be some of your closest friends throughout college and beyond. Although we are a diverse group of individuals, we are connected by our passion and love for technology. We host a variety of social and brotherhood events throughout the semester. These include but are not limited to Formal, Tailgates, Bowling, and Apple picking.
